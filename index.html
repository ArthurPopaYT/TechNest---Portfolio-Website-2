Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the battery life?

The TechNest Pro earbuds offer up to 8 hours of listening time on a single charge, with a total of 30 hours including the charging case. The TechNest Elite extends this to 10 hours with 40 hours total.

Are the earbuds water resistant?

Yes, TechNest Pro earbuds are IPX7 rated, meaning they can withstand immersion in water up to 1 meter for 30 minutes. They're perfect for workouts and can handle rain or sweat without issues.

How effective is the noise cancellation?

Our Active Noise Cancellation technology reduces ambient noise by up to 35dB, effectively eliminating most environmental sounds like airplane cabin noise, office chatter, and traffic. You can toggle between ANC, Transparency mode, and Off.

What's your warranty policy?

All TechNest products come with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Additionally, we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee, allowing you to return for a full refund if you're not completely satisfied.

Can I use just one earbud at a time?

Yes, both earbuds can be used independently in mono mode. Simply keep one in the charging case while using the other.
